Ingredients:
 - 1 lb. potatoes (Yukon Golds or russets are best)
 - 1-1/2 tsp. salt
 - Generous 1/4 tsp. freshly ground black pepper
 - 3 Tbs. vegetable or olive oil for frying; more as needed

Directions:
 1. Peel the potatoes and grate them.
 2. Place in a large bowl
 3. Add the salt and pepper and toss to coat thoroughly
 4. Heat oil in 8-inch skillet over medium-high heat.
 5. When oil is hot, add an even, 1/2 inch thick,
    layer of potatoes
 6. When underside is deep golden brown, flip potatoes
 7. Cook until the new bottom is browned and center potoes
    feel tender when poked with a knife.
 8. Transfer rosti to a cutting board, blot with a paper
    towel to remove excess oil, and serve immediately
